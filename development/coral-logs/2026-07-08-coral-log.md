### Design principle worth cross-pollinating
The strongest product philosophy came out of a *constraint* question
("how do we show depth?") not a feature request. The answer — depth is
felt, never tallied — generalizes: whenever a surface is tempted to
quantify, ask whether the number serves the user or just the system's
convenience. Counting is a trophy in some rooms and a threat in others;
the same digit changes meaning by place.
